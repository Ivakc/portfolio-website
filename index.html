How I Work

THE PROCESS

01

Gather

Collecting fragments — physical, archival, experiential — that orbit an idea before it has a name.

02

Dialogue

Letting clay, wire, textile, and found objects respond to each other in small studies and deliberate failures.

03

Resolve

Building toward a final form that holds both intention and accident in tension.

04

Place

Working with collectors and curators on how each piece inhabits a space over time.
